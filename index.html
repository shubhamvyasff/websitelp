Revolutionizing D2C Inventory Management

The Challenge

We faced significant hurdles with inventory across 50 warehouses, suffering from real-time data inaccuracy, leading to inefficient stock distribution and capital blockages. This challenge hindered growth and market responsiveness, necessitating a data-driven inventory management solution.

The Vision

To implement a real-time inventory management system for accurate, instantaneous warehouse data, enabling informed decision-making and scalability for the growing D2C brand.

Unified Data Platform

  • Integration of diverse data sources for seamless data flow.
  • Real-Time Inventory Dashboard: Comprehensive inventory visibility with automated refreshes, data security, and advanced analytics.
  • Innovative Inventory Strategies: Dynamic thresholds and precision tracking to maintain optimal stock levels using automated inventory rebalancing.

Impactful Results

50% reduction in working capital, improved stock availability, reduced overstock, and enhanced operational efficiency, setting a new standard in inventory management.

Meet the Innovator

His deep knowledge in D2C businesses and expertise in inventory and operations have established him as a key innovator in inventory management, leading Anveshan's system transformation.
